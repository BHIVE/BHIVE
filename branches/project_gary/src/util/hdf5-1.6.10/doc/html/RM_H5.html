H5: General Library Functions

These functions serve general-purpose needs of the HDF5 library and it users.

The C Interfaces:
  • H5open
  • H5close
      
  • H5get_libversion
  • H5check_version
      
  • H5set_free_list_limits
  • H5garbage_collect
